Murder on the Atlantic

A 1920s Lovecraftian Mystery Adventure

Setting

It's 1926, and you find yourself in London, with a desire to travel to the United States. Before your plans have fully formed, an envelope appears under your door: an invitation. Fragile paper is inked in an outdated style, the spidery hand boldly proclaiming your presence is desired upon the Voyager:



The Voyager is a vessel of unmatched class and style, unique in its luxurious and intimate setting.

Expansive storage capacity is available, with the capability for housing all manner of cargo, both mundane and extraordinary.

Complete privacy and discretion provided.

The owner requests only that all passengers dine together each night.

By Invitation Only
